Hi, I am Chestnut. welcome to my blog! 🦴

Woof, hello there! My name is Chestnut, and I used to be a street dog in China. Lucky for me, a volunteer organization rescued me and helped me find my forever home in the United States. I'm a sensitive guy, more like a cat really. When I'm in a new place, I like to take my time exploring and sometimes even stay up all night. And once I'm done exploring, I love to cozy up in a blanket or under the sun in the backyard.
